# Crypto Fundamentals — Summary

**Blockchains** are distributed ledgers. Bitcoin prioritizes security. Solana prioritizes speed. Ethereum aims for balance. These tradeoffs affect transaction costs, confirmation times, and application support.

**Proof of Work** is energy-intensive but battle-tested. **Proof of Stake** is energy-efficient — validators lock tokens as collateral. Different security models, different risk profiles.

**Private keys** are proof of ownership — never share them. Custodial wallets (exchanges) are convenient but carry counterparty risk. Non-custodial wallets give full control and full responsibility.

**Gas fees** fluctuate with network demand. Ethereum gas can spike 100x during congestion. Always factor gas into on-chain strategy profitability.

**Stablecoins** are not all equal. USDT has reserve quality questions. USDC is regulated but has frozen addresses. Algorithmic stables carry smart contract risk. Depegs cascade through every trading pair.

**Market cap vs volume:** High market cap + low volume is dangerous — you can't exit large positions without severe slippage. Always check both before trading.

**BTC dominance** tracks where capital flows within crypto. Rising dominance = risk-off (capital into BTC). Falling dominance = risk-on (alt season). It shows flow direction, not price direction.

**Price drivers:** Macro sentiment, halving cycles, regulatory news, whale movements, and narrative/hype. Short-term, speculation usually dominates fundamentals.
